ADferno: a website designed to demonstrate the dangers of growing commercialization in social media platforms through the saturation and intensification of advertising

As social media platforms grow in power and influence, our digital experiences are increasingly at stake. What we are experiencing is the increasingly harmful socioeconomic effects of the dynamics of the digital economy. Within this capitalized economy, data is prioritized to gain not only profit but control and governance. Once we lose ownership over our own data, we lose all control over our digital lives and the web is no longer a democracy. Decision-making and influence becomes concentrated in the hands of a few rather than free for all.

Our online experiences are now dominated by endless advertisements while concerns of privacy and misinformation heavily burden us. Our speech becomes monetized content as our views become data. Our digital lives have become saturated with advertisements and our presence is commodified.
